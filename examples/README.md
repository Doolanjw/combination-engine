---

## What These Examples Show

**Transfer does not require similarity.** A kingfisher and a bullet train share no obvious connection. The useful link is structural: both face a pressure-differential problem at a medium transition.

**AI does not invent — it reassigns.** In the halicin case, the compound already existed. AI surfaced a non-obvious use by searching across a space no team had reason to search manually.

**The method produces candidates, not answers.** Each example ends with surviving hypotheses and explicit reasons why they deserve testing. None ends with a proven product.

Read the examples before the prompts if the method is new to you.
