The Framework

The five-step Combination Engine method

Most innovation is recombination, not invention from scratch. The barrier is not a shortage of ideas — it is a shortage of visibility across domains. This method gives you a repeatable process for closing that gap.

1

Identify the Friction Point

Articulate the problem clearly without embedding assumptions about what the solution must look like.

2

Express the Contradiction

Clarify which conditions must coexist but currently conflict. This is the move that opens cross-domain search.

3

Cross-Domain Exploration

Investigate how other industries resolved similar structural tensions. Use AI to search at a scale no human searcher can match.

4

Investigate Expired Patents

Examine past attempts that changing circumstances — new materials, new markets, new manufacturing — might now make viable.

5

Apply Rigorous Filtering

Determine what legal, technical, commercial, or implementation obstacles would disqualify each candidate immediately.
